# Privacy Policy Draft (Template)

This document is a project draft and not legal advice.

## 1. Data We Collect
1. Gameplay telemetry (session/move/score/progression events).
2. Technical diagnostics (errors and runtime health metrics).
3. Purchase and entitlement state for in-app products.

## 2. Why We Collect It
1. Operate and improve gameplay quality.
2. Detect stability and production issues.
3. Balance gameplay and progression.
4. Validate purchase and entitlement flows.

## 3. What We Do Not Collect
1. No intentional collection of sensitive personal categories.
2. No ad-profile monetization in the current ad-free strategy.

## 4. Data Sharing
1. Infrastructure/analytics providers may process operational telemetry on our behalf.
2. We do not sell personal data.

## 5. Retention
1. Logs are retained for reliability and product analytics windows.
2. Older telemetry is removed or aggregated by policy.

## 6. User Rights
1. Users can contact support for data-related questions.
2. Data review/deletion flows will be documented with account-system rollout.

- Support email: `support@luminablocks.com`
